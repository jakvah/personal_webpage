What data is beeing recorded and displayed?

The data beeing recorded are from the PopFlash matches that have been added to the database. The values displayed on the scoreboard and on a players profile is the average over all matches. To keep the scoreboard statistically significant, a player must have 5 matches recorded in order to appear on the scoreboard. As a consquence, the total number of players might not match the players that appear on the scoreboard.

The scoreboard is sorted by players K/D Ratios. This decision is based on the fact that this is how real men measure CSGO stats.
